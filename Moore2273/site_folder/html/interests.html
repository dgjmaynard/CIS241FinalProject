Forever Moore Sweets

I enjoy baking so I started my own home-based bakery business, Forever Moore Sweets. I specialize in cakes, cupcakes, cookies, and decorated cookies. Running a business is teaching me a lot. My dream is to someday have a food truck and a brick & mortar building that I own.

Trinity Everett

I am also active in my church, Trinity Episcopal in Everett, where I am involved with many groups, activities and fundraisers. During this time of COVID-19, I have not been able to attend church physically but I stay in contact with many of my church family members via social media and Zoom.


3D Printing Cookie Cutters

As part of my business, I have been learning to use a 3D printer to create my own cookie cutters. I have quite the cookie cutter collection already but I love the process of learning new technologies and putting them to good use. I use CookieCad where I can upload my design and it produces a cookie cutter file that I can upload to my 3D printer.

Coffee Roasting

Since I love coffee so much, I am learning to roast my own coffee beans. I also think it would be a great addition to my bakery. What goes better with baked goods than coffee? Sweet Maria's has many of the supplies, and tutorials that I need to be able to roast from home. They have quite the selection of green coffee beans ready to be roasted. I am learning quite a bit about the roasting process and I love to drink my custom roasted coffee.
